Residential Educator Training Programme 2025

July 13-18, 2025 | Cape Town

Join high school history educators from across Southern Africa and beyond for an intensive week of professional development focused on Holocaust and genocide education. This comprehensive program combines academic learning with practical teaching strategies.

Program Highlights:

  • Interactive workshops with international experts
  • Survivor testimony sessions
  • Pedagogical training in genocide education
  • Networking with educators across the region
  • Resource development and sharing
