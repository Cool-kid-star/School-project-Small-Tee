Understanding the Holocaust

The Holocaust was the systematic, state-sponsored persecution and murder of six million Jews by the Nazi regime and its collaborators. It remains one of the darkest periods in human history.

Plan Your Visit

Visit the museum to learn more, view exhibits, and honor those lost. Open daily from 10AM to 5PM.
